Ultimate Chocolate Dessert Recipe

Description

Luckily, richer, sexier, and more decadent is exactly what this chocolate mocha pot de crème recipe is all about.!

Ingredients

  • 4 ounces bittersweet chocolate, chopped
  • ½ teaspoon instant coffee
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Stir chocolate, instant coffee, and salt together in a medium bowl.
  2. Heat cream, sugar, and vanilla in a saucepan over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it just begins to simmer.
  3. Pour hot cream mixture over chocolate mixture; let sit for 1 minute.
  4. Whisk chocolate and cream mixture for 1 to 2 minutes, until combined.
  5. Pour mixture into four small espresso cups. Tap to remove any air bubbles. Chill thoroughly in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours.
  6. Serve topped with grated chocolate, or add lightly whipped cream and dust with cocoa powder.
